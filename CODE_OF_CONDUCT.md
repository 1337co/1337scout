# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive, evidence-based feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers — for a sensitive report, open a confidential advisory via the repository's **Security → Report a vulnerability** channel, or otherwise contact the maintainers through the repository. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's identity will be kept confidential.
